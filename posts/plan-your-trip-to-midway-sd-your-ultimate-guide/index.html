Plan Your Trip to Midway SD

Published: 2024

Planning a visit to Midway? Here is everything you need for a memorable trip.

Getting Here

Midway is accessible by car via state highways. The nearest major airport is approximately 90 minutes away. Scenic driving routes add to the journey.

Where to Stay

  • Historic bed and breakfasts in restored homes
  • Modern hotels and motels along the highway
  • Campgrounds and RV parks for outdoor enthusiasts

Best Time to Visit

Summer offers the most activities and pleasant weather. Fall brings stunning foliage. Spring is ideal for wildflowers and fewer crowds. Winter provides a cozy small-town holiday experience.

Local Dining

From classic American diners to farm-to-table restaurants, Midway's dining scene celebrates local ingredients and hearty Midwestern hospitality.
