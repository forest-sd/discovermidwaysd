Best Hiking Trails in Midway SD

Published: 2025

Midway and its surrounding areas offer diverse hiking opportunities for all skill levels, from gentle nature walks to challenging backcountry trails.

Popular Trails

  • Prairie Ridge Trail: Easy 2-mile loop with wildflower meadows and bird watching
  • Bluff Overlook Path: Moderate 4-mile out-and-back with panoramic views
  • Creek Bottom Trail: Family-friendly walk along the waterway

Trail Tips

Carry water, wear appropriate footwear, check weather conditions, and tell someone your plans. Most trails are best enjoyed spring through fall.
