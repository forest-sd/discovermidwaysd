Top Fishing Spots Near Midway for Anglers

Published: 2025

The waters around Midway offer excellent fishing opportunities throughout the season.

Top Spots

  • Mill Creek Reservoir: Stocked with walleye and bass, boat launch available
  • Prairie Lake: Excellent catfish and crappie, peaceful shoreline fishing
  • South Branch Creek: Trout fishing in a scenic canyon setting

Fishing Tips

South Dakota fishing licenses are required. Check current regulations at gfp.sd.gov. Best times are early morning and late evening during summer months.
