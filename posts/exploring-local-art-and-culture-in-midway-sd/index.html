Local Art and Culture in Midway SD

Published: 2024

Despite its small-town charm, Midway boasts a surprisingly vibrant arts and culture scene.

Galleries and Studios

  • Prairie Arts Gallery: Rotating exhibits featuring regional artists
  • Studio Row: Working artist studios open for monthly art walks

Performance Arts

The community theater presents several productions annually, and the summer outdoor concert series brings musical talent from across the region. Poetry readings and storytelling events add to the cultural richness.

Public Art

A growing collection of murals and sculptures throughout downtown tells the story of Midway's past and present, creating an outdoor gallery experience.
