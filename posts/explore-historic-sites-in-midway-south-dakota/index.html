Historic Sites in Midway, South Dakota

Published: 2024

Midway's history stretches back to early settlement days, and the community takes pride in preserving its heritage.

Must-Visit Sites

  • Midway Heritage Museum: Comprehensive collection of local artifacts and photographs
  • Pioneer Homestead: Restored 1880s farmstead with guided tours
  • Historic Downtown: Walking tour of original commercial buildings

Historical Events

Annual Heritage Days celebrate Midway's founding with reenactments, traditional crafts, and historical lectures. Check the community calendar for dates.
