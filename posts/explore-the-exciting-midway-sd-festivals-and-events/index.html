Midway SD Festivals and Events

Published: 2024

Midway hosts a vibrant calendar of events that bring the community together year-round.

Annual Events

  • Spring Festival: Kicking off the season with live music, food vendors, and craft booths
  • Summer Concert Series: Free outdoor concerts every Friday in July
  • Harvest Fair: Agricultural exhibitions, pie contests, and carnival rides
  • Winter Wonderland: Holiday lights, tree lighting ceremony, and Santa visits

Farmers Market

Running May through October, the Saturday farmers market features local produce, baked goods, handmade crafts, and live entertainment.
