About Midway

Nestled in the heart of South Dakota, Midway is a vibrant hub of friendly faces, remarkable experiences, and breathtaking landscapes. Whether you are a resident or visitor, there is plenty to love about our charming community.

Our Community

Midway offers an array of outdoor activities including hiking, fishing, and biking. Parks and trails promise fun for the whole family, while our community calendar showcases festivals, farmers markets, and seasonal celebrations.

Rich History

Our history is deeply embedded in the town's charm. Historic sites and museums highlight our journey and the milestones that have shaped our community.
